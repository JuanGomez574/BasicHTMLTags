Favorite NBA Players

Choosing Three

I've been watching the NBA for about 17 years, and I've witnessed so many amazing NBA players. The challenge will be narrowing that list down. I will choose my top three favorites since the time I've been watching.

Top Three

  • Dwyane Wade
  • LeBron James
  • Steph Curry

Dwayne Wade

Growing up, one of my first memories of basketball was when Dwyane Wade carried the Miami Heat in the 2006 NBA Finals 2006 NBA finals, coming back from down 0-2 to win the championship. I also liked his slashing style of basketball.

LeBron James

LeBron is a legendary player, probably the second best ever behind Michael Jordan. When he joined the Miami Heat to form a superteam with Dwayne Wade and Chris Bosh I was thrilled. Then when he came back from being down 1-3 to the Warriors, that really cemented him into one of my favorites.

Steph Curry

He is one of the most exciting players to watch. His exceptional 3-point shooting skills made me a fan.
